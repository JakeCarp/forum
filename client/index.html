The Kings Rules

There shalt be no vulgar language. Respect your fellow forum-goers as you would your king.

Grammar, spelling, and punctuation are important when presenting thyself among the masses within posts or comments. Speak as thou wouldst to a nobleman rather than a peasant.

Commentary on a post is not obligatory. Images art allowed to exist by themselves. Remember, though: A photo without context can be cause for controversy.

Do at least try to keep any photos posted relevant to what thee are speaking of. A photo of a dragon is not indicative of how comfortable thine shoes are. Please keep it relevant.

Revealing personal information, such as where thy dwelling or hovel are, thy name, date of birth, sensitive payment information, and anything else that might be considered integral to thy person, can result in poachers adopting thy identity, which can lead to trouble with the local highwaymen and tax services. Please be aware of what thou art sharing.

Welcome to the Posted King

Here thou art welcome to be whoever thee wants to be, so long as it falls within the guidelines set forth by the creators of this social gathering. Whether thou wishes to speak about thine day, thine pets, the goings-on within the kingdom, local faires, or anything else that interests thee, thine posts are welcome here.

Perhaps thou hast already noticed that there is a hierarchy within the pages of this site. There shall always be a king, who is established by an amalgamation of the likes across one user's posts. If thou are the user whose posts, in total, hast the most likes over anyone else, thou are crowned the king and thine most liked post will be pinned to the top of the page. Truly, an honor in and of itself!

When thou are the king, the amount of likes thee hast across all posts will multiply thine influence upon others. Whereas normal forum-goers hast one 'like' per like, the king's will increment per how many likes he has. Eventually, the king's likes could add 2, 5, 10, or perhaps even 100 likes per like that he bestows.

But beware! The King can change at any moment! Although each individual can only like a post once, the number of likes on another user's overall posts may, at some point, overtake the current King. The crown will then be passed to that person.

And now, I set you free! Be sure to read and follow the rules, and fight to become The Posted King! Maybe the odds be ever in thy favor.
